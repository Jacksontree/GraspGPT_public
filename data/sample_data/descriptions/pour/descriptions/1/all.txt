Household objects that support the function of pouring include utensils such as pitchers, cups and ladles, as well as containers with pouring spouts, to aid in the transfer of liquid or other items from one vessel to another. Verbs that are similar to pour include stream, cascade, trickle, spurt, gush, splash, and flow. They all describe the action of transferring a liquid from one container to another in a force caused by gravity.
