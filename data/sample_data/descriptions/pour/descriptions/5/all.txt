Household objects that can be used to pour include pitchers, measuring cups, and strainers. These objects can be used to pour liquids such as water, milk, juice, and other beverages, as well as solids such as grains, flour, and sugar. Verbs similar to 'pour' include stream, trickle, splash, splatter, spray and cascade, all of which involve rapidly moving a liquid or other substance from one container to another.
