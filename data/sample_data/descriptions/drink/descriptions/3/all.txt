Household objects that can be used to drink from include cups, glasses, mugs, and tumblers with openings that allow for liquid to be transferred from the vessel to the mouth. In addition, specialized tools for drinking such as straws, spoons, and ladles can be used to sip and transfer liquid from the vessel into the mouth. Verbs similar to 'drink' in terms of meaning can include terms such as imbibe, ingest, consume, and swallow, as all of them refer to the action of taking in a liquid, either into the mouth or elsewhere in the body.
