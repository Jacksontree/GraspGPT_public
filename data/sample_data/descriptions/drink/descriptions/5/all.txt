Household objects such as cups, glasses, mugs, and bottles can be used to drink liquid beverages like water, tea, juice and coffee. They come in a variety of shapes and sizes, and can be made from a range of materials such as glass, plastic, and metal. Verbs similar to "drink" include imbibe, quaff, and guzzle, which all involve taking in a beverage, usually through the act of sipping or gulping.
